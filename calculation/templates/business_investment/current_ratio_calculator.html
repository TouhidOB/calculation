Calculate the **Current Ratio**, a key liquidity metric that assesses a company's ability to cover its short-term debt obligations using its short-term assets.

Current Ratio Formula:

$$\text{Current Ratio (Ratio)} = \frac{\text{Total Current Assets}}{\text{Total Current Liabilities}}$$

Balance Sheet Inputs (in $)

$

Cash, accounts receivable, inventory, and other assets convertible to cash within one year.

$

Accounts payable, short-term debt, and other obligations due within one year.
