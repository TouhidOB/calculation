This **Accounts Receivable efficiency ratio** measures the average number of days it takes for a company to collect cash after a credit sale, assessing cash flow management.

DSO Formula:

$$\text{DSO} = \left(\frac{\text{Average Accounts Receivable}}{\text{Net Credit Sales}}\right) \times \text{Days in Period}$$
