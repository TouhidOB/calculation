Earnings Per Share (EPS) Calculator

Calculate the Basic **Earnings Per Share**, the portion of a company's profit allocated to each common stock share. This is a crucial measure of profitability.

Basic EPS Formula:

$$\text{Basic EPS} = \frac{\text{Net Income} - \text{Preferred Dividends}}{\text{Weighted Average Common Shares Outstanding}}$$
