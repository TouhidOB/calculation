This **Acid-Test Ratio** is a strict liquidity measure, checking if a company can meet its immediate short-term obligations using only its most **liquid assets** (excluding inventory and prepaid expenses).

Quick Ratio Formula:

$$\text{Quick Ratio} = \frac{\text{Current Assets} - \text{Inventory} - \text{Prepaid Expenses}}{\text{Current Liabilities}}$$
