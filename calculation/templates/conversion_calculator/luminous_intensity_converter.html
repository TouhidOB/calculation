**Candela Definition:** The candela is the luminous intensity, in a given direction, of a source that emits monochromatic radiation of frequency $540 \times 10^{12} \text{ hertz}$ and that has a radiant intensity in that direction of $1/683 \text{ watt}$ per steradian ($\text{W}/\text{sr}$). Thus, $1 \text{ cd} = 1 \text{ lumen}/\text{steradian}$.
