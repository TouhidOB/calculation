Combination Calculator ($\text{nCr}$)

Calculates the number of ways to select $r$ items from $n$ items, where **order does not matter**.

The Combination Formula:

$$\mathbf{\text{C}(n, r) = \binom{n}{r} = \frac{n!}{r! (n-r)!}}$$
