Sum of Series Calculator ($\mathbf{S_n}$)

Calculate the sum of the first $\mathbf{n}$ terms for Arithmetic and Geometric Progressions.

Arithmetic Series ($S_n$)

Formula: $S_n = \frac{n}{2} [2a + (n-1)d]$

Geometric Series ($S_n$)

Formula: $S_n = a \frac{1 - r^n}{1 - r}$ (for $r \neq 1$)
