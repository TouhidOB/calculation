Understanding the 28/36 Rule

Many lenders follow the "28/36" guideline: your mortgage payment shouldn't exceed **28%** of your gross income, and your total debt shouldn't exceed **36%**. However, programs like FHA often allow up to **43%** or higher with compensating factors.
